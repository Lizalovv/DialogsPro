About.

Dialogs Pro is a user-friendly dialog maker tool, enabling seamless creation of custom alert dialogs and toasts. Tailored for online and offline scenarios, it enhances user interaction. The tool includes essential utilities and supports saving created dialogs in Dex for easy management.

Features.

Custom Alert Dialogs

Users can design personalized alert dialogs, tailoring them to specific needs and preferences.

Versatile Toasts

Create customizable toast messages with various styles and content, enhancing the visual appeal of notifications.

Offline and Online Dialogs

Craft dialogs suitable for both offline and online environments, ensuring flexibility in usage.
